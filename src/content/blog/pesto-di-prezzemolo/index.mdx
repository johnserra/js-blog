Here's a recipe for Pesto di Prezzemolo (Parsley Pesto):

## Ingredients:

* 1 bunch fresh parsley
* 1/3 cup toasted pine nuts
* 2 cloves garlic, chopped
* 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
* 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
* Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions:

1. Wash and dry the parsley leaves and remove any thick stems.
2. In a food processor, combine the parsley, pine nuts, garlic, and Parmesan cheese.
3. Pulse the mixture until it is coarsely chopped.
4. While the food processor is running, slowly add the olive oil until the pesto is smooth and well blended.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Serve immediately, or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Enjoy your Pesto di Prezzemolo!
