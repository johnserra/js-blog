The traditional gremolata recipe consists of three main ingredients: finely chopped fresh parsley, grated lemon zest, and minced garlic. The ratio is typically 1:1:1. Mix the ingredients together and use it as a garnish or seasoning for dishes such as osso buco or roasted meats.
