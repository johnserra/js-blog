Here is a traditional recipe for tabbouleh:

## Ingredients:

* 1/2 cup bulgur wheat
* 1 cup chopped parsley
* 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint
* 2 tomatoes, diced
* 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
* 3 tablespoons olive oil
* 3 tablespoons lemon juice
* Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions:

1. Rinse the bulgur wheat in cold water and drain well.
2. Soak the bulgur wheat in 1 cup of cold water for 30 minutes, until it has absorbed all the water and is tender.
3. In a large bowl, mix together the chopped parsley, fresh mint, diced tomatoes, and finely chopped red onion.
4. Add the cooked bulgur wheat to the bowl and mix well.
5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Pour the dressing over the salad and mix well.
7. Cover and refrigerate for at least an hour before serving to allow the flavors to blend. Enjoy!
